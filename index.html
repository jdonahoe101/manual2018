Daily Schedule

Once you have worked here for a while, you most likely will develop your own routine that works better for you then the one I have become accustomed to. Until then, please make use of the one that is listed here and make sure everything is done on time.

  • Every day by 3, make sure to start the dishwasher.
  • Empty the dishwasher by the time you leave.
  • At the end of each day clean off all the tables and counters with clorox wipes.
  • Clean out the Coffee holder.
  • Water all the plants on Friday
  • Clean out the fridge on Friday
  • Check the mail each day

Post Office

EOSG MAIL: Outgoing mail such as bills, paystubs, and paychecks are to be dropped off inside the building at 1331 Pin Oak Road no later than 4:30PM

CERTIFIED MAIL:This green form allows the sender proof of mailing as well as proof of delivery. Certified mail also allows the sender to receive a copy of the recipients signature at the time of delivery.

CERTIFICATE OF MAILING: Certificates of mailing allows the sender to obtain proof of sent mail. You will occasionally be asked to fill these out. When you do you need to take them to the post office located at 5701 4th street, Old Katy.
